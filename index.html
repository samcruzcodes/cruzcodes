    About

    Scroll or click through to learn a bit about me and my journey with tech!

    2015 (9 years old)

    I stumbled upon a youtube video about a Minecraft multiplayer server. From that moment on I was captivated. I begged my mom to buy me Minecraft so I could play. If only she knew this would be the start of my journey with tech.

    2016-2018 (tween)

    Having fell in love with videogames I wanted to do what every other kid wanted to do, be a YouTuber. Although, it wasn't successful I learned alot about editing and optimization with my family computer.

    2018 (12 Years Old)

    Having done everything possible with optimizing the family computer, it was time I just upgraded. I binged so many Linus Tech Tips videos, easily watching over 1000 hours before playing with expensive legos (building my pc).

    2020 (14 Years Old)

    The year of quarantine was the year of me diving into 3d printing. Having stumbled upon a 3d printed boat on TikTok I HAD to learn more. On July 12th I built my Ender 3 Pro. Since then, I've created an entire shelf worth of prints.

    2021-2023 (15-16)

    I was tasked to spend 25 hours on a project for school and I thought it would be perfect to make a game. Over 500 hours of graphic designing and coding for the first time later: Turtleburg! My edutainment RPG about the water crisis.

    2023 (17 Years Old)

    After Turtleburg, I wanted to dive into other parts of CS. So, I signed up for Kode With Klossy for the summer not expecting to like web development much but ended up coming out with three languages and "EdVisor".

    2023 (17 Years Old)

    Having finished KWK, I was inspired to do web development on my own. I wanted to start simple, my own website. That in which you are scrolling through right now, Thank You!

    To be Determined

    This is only the beginning, I have a long way to go before I make a real name for myself in computer science. Next steps: Girls Who Code!
